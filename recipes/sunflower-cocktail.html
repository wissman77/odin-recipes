Sunflower Cocktail

Description

Easy sunflower cocktail recipe using equal parts of each ingredient.

Ingredients

  • 2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers gin (such as Aviation速 or Hendrick's速)
  • 2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers orange-flavored liqueur (such as Cointreau速)
  • 2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers elderflower liqueur (such as St. Germain速)
  • 2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers lemon juice
  • crushed ice

Steps

  1. Combine gin, orange-flavored liqueur, elderflower liqueur, and lemon juice with some crushed ice in a cocktail shaker. Cover and shake until the outside of the shaker has frosted.
  2. Strain into 2 glasses.
